BLOG TRAIN DAY 3: Ramona reveals motivation for ‘Redeemed by Grace’

April 24, 2015

Today’s blog train/tour “boxcar” brings us to the doorstep of the subject of the book, “Redeemed by Grace: A Catholic Woman’s Journey to Planned Parenthood and Back”: Ramona herself! Yep, she’s peeling back the layers of why she didn’t just keep the story that became a book contained to thoughts in the recesses of her mind; why she reordered her life for a couple years to turn part of it into a series of chapters you can now hold in your hands.

Ramona has a beautiful way of expressing her thoughts. She’s a writer herself; just one too busy as a mother of little ones right now to stop long enough to finish a whole book, which is why I stepped in — and I’m glad to have had the chance.

So, without further adieu, take it away Ramona! (Read her ruminations here today: http://sidewalkadvocates.org/category/blog/)
