7 quick takes friday (vol. 6)

May 1, 2009

Here’s something a little different. I’m going to try to actually be quick with my “quick takes” this time. Since I’m starting after midnight, I think I might actually accomplish this! For more “quick takes,” see Conversion Diary.

—1—

You know it’s been a busy week when…

—2—

Here’s how my trip to the Tide “Loads of Hope” offering ended. Can any mother out there imagine a more wonderful gift than to drop off your dirty laundry one afternoon, and return the next and have it being handed back to you clean, sorted, bagged and with a bow on top? Yay Tide! For more, go here and here.

—3—

Heard earlier this week on the radio:

Q:

“What is wrong with the world?”

A:

“I am.” (G.K. Chesterton)

What an answer! Something to ponder. Isn’t it so much easier to point the finger at others? But who, of all the millions of people in the world, can we truly change? And who among us has made the most mistakes that directly affect us? (moi!) Alright then, we know what we’ve got to do to make the world a better place.
