July 3, 2009

–1–

WHAT THE KIDS ARE UP TO:

To uncover the mystery of this strangely-titled sign, read my previous post, and if you feel inclined, please drop back to share memories of your childhood club-house adventures.

–2–

WHAT I’M READING: What Remains: A Memoir of Fate, Friendship and Love, by Carole Radziwill, widow of Prince Anthony Radziwill, who was a cousin to John Kennedy Jr. The memoir details the tragic summer when Carole lost her husband and two dear friends in quick succession, as well as some of the years leading up to that fateful time in her life. I’m finding it to be a poignant and well-written account.

–3–

WHAT’S UP WITH THIS TWITTER THING ANYWAY?
