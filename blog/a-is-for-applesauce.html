A Is For Applesauce

September 26, 2008


What my kindergartener did today:

Take a bin of shiny apples…

Put them on a peeling press and twirl the handle round and round…

Watch the skin curl and fall away…


Throw the apple insides into a Crockpot…

And wait. Several hours later, tasty applesauce will be the reward. Until then, learn about the seasons of the apple tree and draw them: winter nakedness, pink blossoms in spring, leaves and buds in summer, and at the end of the cycle, juicy apples. A present from nature!
