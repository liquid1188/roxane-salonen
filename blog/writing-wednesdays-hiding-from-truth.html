writing wednesdays: hiding from truth

April 18, 2013

I’ve got my flashlight out today, and I’m shining a light on a situation that most of us wish wasn’t so.

It’s Writing Wednesday and my post today (yes, you’re to go read it!) touches on the responsibility we have as communicators, shared in the framework of the Kermit Gosnell case.

We’ve pushed away from truth for too long in this case. Time to get those lights burning so we can move through this and come out better on the other side.
