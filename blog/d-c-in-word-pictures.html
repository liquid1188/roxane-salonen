D.C. in Word Pictures

January 30, 2013

I’m almost always caught by words; words that say much with few letters, poetic words from the heart, bold statements meant to prompt action.

While in D.C. this past week/end, I bumped into my fair share of words. Many made me pause. Often, they uplifted. Some were even life-changing.

I’ll keep my own words to a minimum this post to allow you a visual tour of some of the words I met while in the nation’s capital.
