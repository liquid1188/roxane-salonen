It’s a fine day for a giveaway! (Catholic Mom’s Prayer Companion)

September 7, 2016

I love to give away joy. When something excites me, I want others to know. When something edifies me and adds meaning and beauty to my life, it seems a sin to not share.

Every once in a while something really special comes along that I am in the privileged position to pass on to others. After months of anticipation, the 80 contributors to this new little gem from Ave Maria Press, “The Catholic Mom’s Prayer Companion: A Book of Daily Reflections,” are celebrating through the miles, giving one another digital High-5s, and squealing with delight at the opportunity to share a book chocked full of inspiration with the rest of the world. And yes, I am among them!

If you’ve heard about it and wanted a copy, today, you’ll have a chance to win one. If you haven’t but are curious, below are a few tidbits to tempt you into vying for your chance to win a copy. I promise, it’s super easy to be added to the drawing. Just leave a comment below about why you think you would enjoy this book, and I’ll announce the winner a week from today and send out your copy. (Make sure to return in a week to see if you won and also, so I can connect with you if you turn up the winner so I can get the book to you!) Easy peasy. And I think you’ll be tickled to have a copy. I know that I am enjoying it immensely as a new part of my morning prayer.

Recently, I did a podcast interview with Allison Gingras which touches on the work, along with other facets of my life as a praying mother and writer.
