Lazy Days of Summer

June 1, 2008

One of my favorite days of the year is the first spring outing at Fargo’s Lindenwood Park. I’ve now spent several glorious afternoons there, and now that my little ones are big enough to not need me to help them every second, I can actually sit on a bench and keep an eye on them while soaking up a little sun. It’s restorative, to say the least. During one of our recent trips there, I brought my camera and enjoyed snapping a few shots from below.

It’s been a while since I’ve posted. A trip back to my hometown in Montana had me out out of commission for a while. It’s good to be back in some kind of routine, although…I’m not sure it’s been completely established yet. Yes, we’re in transition, but so far so good.

And now, with summer upon us, who knows what adventures will unfold?
