memento mondays: nuptials, schenker and snowflake guzzling

October 29, 2012

Week’s beginning sunrise…took my breath away!

My sweet communications assistant ties the knot!
Girly-girl daughter in her fufu dress by the cake table
Looking down from the 4th floor of the Radisson at a circle of visitors
Hubby meets longtime hero, Michael Schenker
Yep, the election is coming! Clever cartoon spotted in The Forum
Snowflake-guzzlers caught in the action, tongues fully engaged
A fresh layer of snow outside, the cats have it right: time to get cozy!
