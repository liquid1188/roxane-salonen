The Drawer that Made All the Difference

July 11, 2012

Everything was feeling pretty good about my new office space, but something tugged at me. Was this really my space?

But then, in the middle of a harried day of training and totally by chance, it caught my eye.

In the back nook where supplies are stored, among all the reams of paper, copy machine and assorted files…

Yes, it even included an exclamation point. (!)

That’s when I knew I was in the right place. My predecessor had designated a specific drawer — one smack dab in the center of everything — as the chocolate drawer.

I had found my true home. There was no longer any question: this change was going to be alright.

My life…is at last complete.


Nothing can stop me now!
