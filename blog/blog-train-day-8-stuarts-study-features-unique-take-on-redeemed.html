BLOG TRAIN DAY 8: ‘Stuart’s Study’ features unique take on ‘Redeemed’

April 29, 2015

We’ve finally reached the tail of our blog train/tour for “Redeemed by Grace: A Catholic Woman’s Journey to Planned Parenthood and Back,” and waving from the caboose, we’ve got blogger Stuart of “Stuart’s Study,” who writes book reviews on Catholic and Orthodox literature and material.

I’m looking forward to reading Stuart’s take on the story of Ramona Trevino’s conversion. Aren’t you? Here’s where you can find it.

Thanks for traveling with us on this ride. It’s been a joy! I hope by now that you’ve either read or are interested in reading Ramona’s story, and that when you do, you are met with a heart-filled account that promises to bring good to our world.

God bless!
