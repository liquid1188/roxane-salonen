Franny’s Fly-In

April 15, 2008

Four Tuesdays have passed since my last Forum column. With my latest turn, I’ve taken up the subject of how I learned more about the depths of a mother’s heart from a house finch named Franny. Above are photos from my brief time with Franny and her babies. Read about Franny here: http://www.in-forum.com/columnists/ (Hint: you’ll have to scroll down the list of columnists to find me.)

Previously published column articles from the Forum’s “Parenting Perspectives” pages can be found here, including my first three and those of the other columnists: http://www.in-forum.com/collections/index.cfm?collection=parenting. So far, I’ve covered depression, breastfeeding and the sighting of a laundry critter.

Fly high in your parenting this week!
