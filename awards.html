Awards & honors.

Roxane’s books and journalism have been honored by the Catholic Media Association, the National Federation of Press Women, and the North Dakota Professional Communicators.

Finding Flannery · 2026

Catholic Media Association Book Awards

  • 2nd place, Memoir — 2026 Catholic Media Association awards competition.
  • A second honor will be bestowed this fall at the 2026 National Federation of Press Women (NFPW) conference and awards banquet in Baltimore.
What Would Monica Do? · 2023

Catholic Media Association Book Awards

  • 1st place in two categories — Parish Life and Family Life, 2023 CMA awards.
  • 1st place, North Dakota Press Women (state level) and 2nd place, National Federation of Press Women (national level).
Roxane B. Salonen · 2026

Communicator of Achievement nominee

  • Nominated by the North Dakota Professional Communicators for the National Federation of Press Women’s highest member honor.
  • Roxane will be featured with fellow nominees at the NFPW national conference in Baltimore, September 2026.
Roxane’s earlier work — including P is for Peace Garden: A North Dakota Alphabet and two decades of columns and features — has also earned state and national press recognition, including honors from the North Dakota Professional Communicators and the National Federation of Press Women.

What colleagues say

Bill Marcil Jr. · CEO & Publisher, Forum Communications Company

Ramona Treviño · co-author, Redeemed by Grace

Danielle Teigen · Features Editor, The Forum
