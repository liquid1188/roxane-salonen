The newest book

Finding Flannery

“She draws large and startling figures — and so does grace.”

A travel memoir tracing the mystique of Southern Gothic writer Flannery O'Connor and the legacy she left us all — co-written with Christina Brajkovich and Karen Anne Mahoney. Winner of a 2026 Catholic Media Association Book Award (2nd place, memoir), with a National Federation of Press Women honor to follow this fall.
